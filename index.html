LAMBORGHINI BOLLETTINO -April 25, 2024

DIREZIONE COR TAURI,

ANOTHER STEP FORWARD



Automobili Lamborghini is continuing its path to decarbonization with a new ambitious
target,supported by the biggest investment in the history of the company. The “Direzione
Cor Tauri” strategy, which involves electrification of the model range and decarbonization
of the production site, has extended its commitment to emissions reductions across the
entire value chain.





TOD's X AUTOMOBILI LAMBORGHINI

Automobili Lamborghini and Tod's present the first footwear collection born from the two
brands' partnership. The exclusive collaboration of the two companies celebrates crafts
-manship and innovation: qualities that define the two companies, each a leader in its
industry.






LAMBORGHINI UNVEILS THE TELEMETRY
X CONCEPT AT CES IN LAS VEGAS

Automobili Lamborghini presents Telemetry X, an innovative 'track connectivity' concept
that can be applied to the future range of super sports cars produced in Sant'Agata
Bolognese. The unveiling took place at the Las Vegas Consumer Electronics Show (CES)
at the Accenture Innovation Hub.






LAMBORGHINI SIGNS THE RENEWAL OF
THE CORPORATE SUPPLEMENTARY
AGREEMENT

Automobili Lamborghini has officially signed the renewal of the Corporate Supplementary
Contract, four years after the last one. A comprehensive revision of working hours and 500
permanent hires by 2026.






URUS PERFORMANTE ENTERS SERVICE
WITH DUBAI POLICE

The Lamborghini Dubai dealership continues its successful collaboration with Dubai Police,
delivering an Urus Performante before the end of 2023, the year that marked its 60th
anniversary.





AUTOMOBILI LAMBORGHINI: OVER 10,000
CARS DELIVERED

In the year 2023 that marked the 60th anniversary of its foundation, Automobili Lamborghini
set a record that will live in the company's history. For the first time, the House of Sant'Agata
Bolognese delivered more than 10,000 cars in a single year.





LARGEST INVESTMENT IN THE
AUTOMOBILI LAMBORGHINI'S HISTORY

Automobili Lamborghini's ambitious Direzione Cor Tauri program, the plan to electrify future
models and decarbonize the entire value chain, supported by the largest investment in the
company's history, continues to grow.
